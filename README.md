# 🛡️ ScamShield

## AI-Powered Scam Detection Assistant

ScamShield is an AI-powered application designed to help users identify suspicious messages and potential scams using Generative AI.

It analyzes scam patterns, phishing attempts, fake offers, and fraudulent messages to provide users with risk insights and safety recommendations.

---

# 🚨 Problem Statement

With the rapid growth of digital communication, online scams have become increasingly common.

Users frequently encounter:

- Phishing emails
- Fake bank messages
- UPI fraud attempts
- Lottery scams
- Fake rewards and offers
- Social engineering attacks

Many people are unable to identify these threats before sharing sensitive information, leading to financial losses and privacy risks.

---

# 💡 Solution

ScamShield acts as an intelligent AI-powered scam detection assistant.

Users can submit suspicious messages, and ScamShield analyzes the content to identify possible scam indicators, explain warning signs, and provide safety recommendations.

The goal is to help users make safer decisions before interacting with suspicious content.

---

# ✨ Features

## 📝 AI Text Scam Detection

- Analyze suspicious SMS, emails, and chat messages
- Detect phishing patterns and scam indicators
- Identify warning signs
- Provide AI-generated explanations
- Give safety recommendations


## 🖼️ Screenshot Scam Analysis

- Support for suspicious screenshot analysis
- Image-based scam detection planned as a future enhancement


---

# ⚙️ How ScamShield Works
User enters suspicious message
↓
Gemini AI analyzes the content
↓
Scam patterns and warning signs are identified
↓
Risk assessment and safety advice are generated
